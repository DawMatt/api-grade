**Resolved** (`specs/007-ai-support/tasks.md` T061–T064): the structured `RULESET_AUTH_FAILED` response already included the four `recoveryOptions` (added in T030), but nothing in the response or in the tool descriptions told the calling AI what to *do* with them — it was free to interpret the failure however it liked, and in this run it chose to silently grade with the built-in ruleset and disclose that only after the fact, instead of surfacing the choice to the user.
